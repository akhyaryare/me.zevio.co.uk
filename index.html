My Story

Why I built this.

The honest version.

I'm not a developer. I've never written a line of production code in my life — until now. I built Zevio using Claude Code, an AI coding tool, and deployed it live to real infrastructure on zevio.co.uk.

The idea came from watching local businesses around me — restaurants, barbers, salons — struggle with the same problems. Missed calls. No-shows. Zero online presence. Manual everything. They're great at what they do, but technology has left them behind.

I believe the next generation of founders won't need to code. They need to understand the problem, have the vision, and know how to use the tools available. That's what I'm proving with Zevio.

We're early. We're building. And we're going after a market that's been ignored for too long.
